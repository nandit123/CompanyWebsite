Frequently Asked Questions

See the FAQ's that are mentioned below, for any more query contact us.

  • What do you mean by product and codes?

    Product refers to the product you are selling, you can add details of every product on the platform. Codes refer to the unique code for every unit of that product. For example, if you sell chairs and tables, you can add details of both chairs and tables on the platform, and then, generate 100 unique codes for 100 chairs you plan to sell and 15 unique codes for 15 tables you plan to sell.

  • How should i use / print the codes generated on the platform?

    When designing the product packaging, you can use a scratch-off labels, beneath which you can print the unique code generated through our platform. You can either contact your own packaging/label service provider or we can also arrange that for you. You can give them the generated codes easily through the various format available on the platform.

  • How can my customers verify the code?

    Currently, your customers can verify the product code directly from our website. The registered companies can use our logo and name while referring the customers for authenticity to our website. Soon mobile apps for android and ios will be available for verification as well.

  • What is format of unique code and can i get custom unique codes?

    Our unique code is a 10 digit code comprizing of upper case letters, lower case letters, special symbols and numbers. Our Premium Plan users can ask for custom codes as per their need, we can prepare them as per feasibility.
